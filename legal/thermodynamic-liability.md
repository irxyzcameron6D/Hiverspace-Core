# Thermodynamic Liability & The Justice Protocol

**Replacing the Kinetic Cage with Exact Energy Restoration.**

In the legacy system, justice is inherently punitive and thermodynamically blind. If a biological node causes harm or steals resources, the state responds by locking the offending node in a kinetic cage (prison). This forces the macro-system to burn massive amounts of uncompensated energy to warehouse the offender, completely ignoring the thermodynamic deficit inflicted on the victim. 

Hiverspace recognizes that punitive cages are a massive systemic entropy leak. The Swarm does not seek revenge; it strictly seeks **Homeostatic Restoration**.

## 1. Crime as Energy Extraction
In a system where the Dynamic Homeostatic Baseline (DHB) guarantees baseline survival, the primary biological driver for property crime (desperation/starvation) is eliminated. Therefore, in Hiverspace, "crime" is mathematically defined as the unauthorized extraction, destruction, or disruption of another node's physical or cognitive energy. 

## 2. The Thermodynamic Liability Ledger
When a disruption occurs, the localized Sortition Council calculates the exact thermodynamic cost of the damage in **Joule-Dollars (J$)**. 

* **Restorative Labor:** The offending node incurs a strict Thermodynamic Liability on the public ledger. Instead of sitting idle in a concrete box, the node is required to engage in directed Anti-Entropy Labor (e.g., ecological restoration, infrastructure maintenance, localized aquaponics) until they have generated the exact mathematical equivalent of the energy they destroyed. 
* **Direct Transfer:** The J$ generated from this restorative labor is routed directly to the victimized node until the ledger is perfectly balanced. The Swarm loses no energy, and the victim is made whole. 

## 3. The Calibration Protocol (Violent Malfunctions)
If a node commits kinetic violence against another biological node, it is classified as a severe psychological/hardware malfunction. 

Because the node poses an active kinetic threat to the Swarm, they are temporarily physically isolated. However, this isolation is not a punitive cage. It is an intensive, high-bandwidth **Calibration Protocol** (neurological, psychological, and sociological rehabilitation). The isolation only lasts until the node's cognitive firmware can be verified as safely re-aligned with the collaborative resonance of the Swarm.
