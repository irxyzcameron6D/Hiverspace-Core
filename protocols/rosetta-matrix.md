## The 3-Step Translation Algorithm

If you are proposing a new mechanism for Hiverspace, run your concept through this mental subroutine before submitting a pull request to the repository:

**Step 1: Strip the Kinetic Payload.** Does your concept rely on violence, coercion, or the threat of starvation to function? If yes, it is a legacy concept. Discard the coercive element. 

**Step 2: Identify the Energy Transfer.**
Look at the core of your idea. Where is the physical or cognitive energy coming from, and where is it going? (For example, if you are translating the legacy concept of "Taxes," the fundamental energy transfer is a portion of localized labor being routed to the macroscopic Swarm for infrastructure). 

**Step 3: Map to the Topology.**
Once you have isolated the raw energy transfer, name it using the fluid dynamics or homeostatic architecture of the Swarm. (Following the previous example, "Taxes" becomes "Macroscopic Baseline Routing").
