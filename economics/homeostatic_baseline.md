# The Dynamic Homeostatic Baseline (DHB)

**The Thermodynamic Floor of Human Survival.**

In the legacy capitalist system, human biological vulnerability (starvation, exposure, lack of medical care) is weaponized as the primary mechanism of coercion. The system uses the continuous threat of death to force the biological nodes into high-friction, low-yield kinetic labor. This traps the human amygdala in a perpetual state of survival panic, destroying the cognitive bandwidth required for high-level, cooperative anti-entropy labor.

The Swarm does not use starvation as a whip. To reach a Kardashev Type I civilization, the biological panic must be permanently engineered out of the system. 

## 1. The Physics of the Baseline
The Dynamic Homeostatic Baseline (DHB) is not a "Universal Basic Income" (UBI) based on arbitrary fiat currency. It is a strict, localized mathematical calculation of the energy required to keep a human biological node in a state of optimal homeostasis.

The DHB calculates the localized thermodynamic cost of:
* **Biological Fuel (Nutrition):** ~10 Megajoules per day (~2400 kcal) of nutrient-dense intake.
* **Thermal Regulation (Shelter):** The kilowatt-hours required for HVAC, lighting, and baseline localized infrastructure.
* **Cellular Maintenance (Healthcare):** The energy and chemical resources required to repair biological entropy (disease/injury).

## 2. The Smart-Contract Distribution
The DHB operates as an immutable smart-contract on the Hiverspace ledger. 

As the decentralized global grid generates real thermodynamic wealth (solar, fusion, geothermal), the network automatically mints and routes the exact baseline requirement in **Joule-Dollars (J$)** to the digital wallet of every registered, living biological node. 

* **The Rule of Unconditionality:** The DHB cannot be revoked, taxed, or garnished by any governing body or legacy debt collector. It is the absolute, unbreachable thermodynamic floor of a human life. 

## 3. The Result: Collaborative Resonance
By guaranteeing the homeostatic floor, Hiverspace neutralizes the apex predators of the legacy system. When a node no longer has to fight its neighbor for the right to exist, it naturally transitions out of *Competitive Entropy* and into *Collaborative Resonance*. 

With baseline survival secured, the massive surplus of human cognitive energy is finally freed to engage in the work that actually matters: raising children, building localized community routers, engineering sustainable grids, and exploring the cosmos.
