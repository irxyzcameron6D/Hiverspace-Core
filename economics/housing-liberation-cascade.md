## THE PROTOCOL — HOW IT WORKS

### Step 1 — Node Registration

Any living human node anywhere on the planet can register as a founding Swarm node by:

1. Downloading the Hiverspace wallet application
2. Completing biometric registration (fingerprint or equivalent)
3. Verifying unique registration (no duplicate registrations)
4. Setting up automatic monthly contribution of 10 J$ (approximately $10 USD at current exchange rates)

Registration is free. The wallet is free. The only cost is the 10 J$/month contribution to the housing fund.

### Step 2 — Fund Accumulation

Monthly contributions from all founding nodes flow automatically via smart contract to the Housing Liberation Fund wallet. The fund balance is publicly visible on the Swarm ledger in real time. Any node can verify the current balance at any time. The fund cannot be accessed by any individual, organization, or government — it is controlled entirely by the housing lottery smart contract.

### Step 3 — Mortgage Selection

The housing lottery smart contract selects mortgages for purchase according to the following priority algorithm:

```
Priority 1: Nodes with highest rent-to-income ratio
            (nodes most stressed by housing costs relative
            to their total energy expenditure)

Priority 2: Nodes with longest continuous contribution history
            (rewards commitment to the cascade)

Priority 3: Geographic distribution weighting
            (ensures cascade spreads globally rather than
            concentrating in specific regions)

Priority 4: Random selection among equally prioritized nodes
            (prevents gaming of the selection criteria)
```

The algorithm is encoded in the smart contract and is publicly auditable. No human makes individual mortgage selection decisions. The algorithm does.

### Step 4 — Mortgage Purchase

When a node is selected and sufficient funds are available the smart contract:

1. Contacts the mortgage holder (bank, private lender, or secondary market holder) with a purchase offer
2. The offer is at market rate — the Swarm does not lowball or negotiate aggressively in early phases
3. As Swarm purchasing power grows, collective bargaining improves offer terms
4. Upon acceptance, the mortgage is transferred to the Housing Liberation Trust
5. The Trust holds the mortgage and the freed node makes no further payments
6. The freed node receives a Liberation Certificate recorded on the Swarm ledger

### Step 5 — The Freed Node Protocol

Upon liberation the freed node:

1. Receives notification of liberation via Swarm wallet
2. Automatically enrolled in the 100 J$/month contribution schedule (begins Month 1 after liberation)
3. Receives the Freed Node Recruitment Kit — tools for recruiting two new founding nodes
4. Joins the Freed Node Network — a community of nodes who have been through liberation and support those going through it
5. Liberation status recorded permanently on Swarm ledger as proof of work

**The freed node's ongoing obligations are minimal and meaningful:**
- 100 J$/month to the housing fund (a fraction of former rent)
- Recruit two new founding nodes in the first year
- Nothing else. No conditions. No compliance requirements. No ideology. Just the contribution and the recruitment.

### Step 6 — The Proof of Work

The freed node is the proof of work.

Not a cryptographic hash. Not a mathematical puzzle. A person. Someone their neighbors know. Someone who used to pay rent and now does not. Someone who can point to a liberation certificate on the Swarm ledger and say: this is real, this works, this is what ten dollars a month from ten million people actually does.

This is economic democracy. Not the theoretical kind. The operational kind where the people who buy the cars own a piece of the company that makes them and have a legally enforceable say in how it is run.
